Overview

hi there!

Welcome to my website. I am a maker at heart and like teaching functional prototyping involving mechatronic design and fabrication in fun ways :).

Research wise, I am interested to design and develop products/solutions to improve the quality of life of people. I would like to focus on design problems in health care and our habitat in the form of buildings and the cities that we live in.

I would also be interested in the development of low cost prototyping machines for making makerspaces more accesible.

Please feel free to explore other areas of my website.
