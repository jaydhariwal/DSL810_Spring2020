Hi! I like teaching functional prototyping involving mechatronic design and fabrication in fun ways :).

Research and practice wise, I am interested in design for wellness in our habitat (in the form of buildings and the cities that we live in). I feel design research to be an interdisciplinary process and I love to work at the intersection of these areas.

Please feel free to explore other areas of the website.
