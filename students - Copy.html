Potential PhD Students

For PhD admissions for the autumn semester 2020, students who are passionate about impactful research in the area of low energy, low cost cooling are strongly encouraged to apply. Please go through the need and the solution criteria of the global cooling prize as a reference. This research work has the potential to improve the quality of life of billions of people from around the world who don't have access to affordable cooling and are at the risk of exposure to increasing levels of heat stress.


It would greatly help if the student has a background in hands-on mechatronics and experimental side of thermal and fluid engineering. The student should be interested in applying design thinking and interdisciplinary learning as per the needs of the research problem.


The Department of Design shortlisting criteria can be found on the website . If you are interested to apply, please go to this IIT Delhi online portal . The last date for submitting the application for admission in the spring semester using the online portal is 31st October, 2019. Please email me at jay@design.iitd.ac.in for questions.

Please note that the PhD positions are open for candidates from all around the world. Please feel free to browse my website for knowing more about my work. Thank you.
