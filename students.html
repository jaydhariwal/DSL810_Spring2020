Potential PhD Students

For PhD admissions for the autumn semester 2020, students who are passionate about impactful research at the intersection of IoT based design and design for wellness in the built environment are highly encouraged to apply.


The last date for application for PhD admissions for the autumn semester 2020 for our department at IIT Delhi is 30th March, 2020. Details for application and shortlisting criteria can be found at this link. Some of the research directions that could be explored are:
1. Ventilation and cooling design to prevent airborne transmission of SARS-COV-2 in health care facilities and other indoor spaces
2. Passive solar architecture and COVID-19
3. Medical device R&D for COVID-19
4. AI/ML/data science for COVID-19
5. Resilient health systems (lessons from COVID-19)


Please note that the PhD positions are open for candidates from all around the world. Please feel free to browse my website for knowing more about my work. Thank you.
